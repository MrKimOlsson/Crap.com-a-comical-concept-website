    History of crap

    One mans crap, another mans banana.

    The founder of Crap, Harry Schits was a poor young boy without a dime. But one day a salesman threw a banana at him. He sat there as looked at the salesman, selling compleat crap and yet people would buy it. The banana was worth more he thought. Then Harry realized that if he could make people believe that they needed something they did´nt really need. He could sell anything. Even this banana! For one man it might be crap, but for another..

    From that day Harry started collecting all kind´s of crap to sell. He even sold that banana, and all the crap he could come across ever since then. And ever since our catchphrase has always been..

    "Need some crap? We got your back!".
